## Sports Visualizations Made Easy

Fanalyzer puts easy, interactive visualizations at your fingertips.

Our visualization tools will help you efficiently share data that is visually pleasing and insightful.

Visualization types:

- Scatterplot 
- Bar Graph
- Histogram
- Pie Chart
